# Terms of service

The published terms are served at `https://<WebDomain>/terms.html`. **The
source of truth is
[`infra/web-assets/terms.html`](../infra/web-assets/terms.html)** — edit it
there and redeploy `S2cWeb-{stage}` to publish changes.

Key points: AI extraction is best-effort (users must verify important dates),
acceptable-use covers deliberately misleading corrections (which the
poisoning defenses also handle technically), and the service is provided
as-is at personal scale.
